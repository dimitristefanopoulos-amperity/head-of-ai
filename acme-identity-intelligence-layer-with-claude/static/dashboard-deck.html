Executive Summary

Unified Shopper Identity

Amperity has resolved fragmented customer data across Acme digital and physical channels into a single unified shopper identity. E-commerce, in-store POS, mobile app, and email engagement records are now connected to deliver a true 360-degree customer view.

Cross-Brand Recognition

Identity resolution now spans both Acme and Bloomingdale's namespaces, enabling cross-brand customer recognition. Shared shoppers are identified and deduplicated while preserving brand-specific engagement histories and preference signals.

Loyalty Program Optimization

Star Rewards loyalty members are matched across all touchpoints, eliminating duplicate accounts and ensuring accurate point accrual and tier classification. Loyalty coverage has expanded through identity-linked attribution of previously anonymous transactions.
